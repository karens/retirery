Volunteer as a tax preparer

AARP Foundation Tax-Aide offers free tax-filing help to those who need it most. They’re looking for compassionate and friendly individuals to join their team of local volunteers for the tax season. You’ll receive training and continued support in a welcoming environment. And you’ll not only learn new skills, but also get a great feeling from helping someone else.

This is a volunteer opportunity that could also provide the background you need for getting paid work doing income tax preparation.
