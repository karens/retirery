Beware of Bias in Retirement Financial Advice

Biased retirement investment advice could be costing retirees millions of dollars. At retirement, most people have accumulated more money that at any other time in their life, and they suddenly must make some sophisticated financial decisions about all that money. But financial advisors are not required to act as fiduciaries (act in the best interest of their clients).
